About Us

Care Club, a non-profit organization in india is to empower underprivileged children, youth and women through relevant education, innovative healthcare and market-focused livelihood programmes.

Mission

The mission of Care club is to enhance lives of children through improved access to Education, Nutrition, Healthcare, Water, Sanitation Services and contribution to environmental conservation.

Vission

Our Vision is the empowerment of communities through eradication of poverty.
